Drop in the source documents — filings, reports, transcripts, spreadsheets. Any format, any scale.

## Step 02 — Define

Spell out what the report should contain — the fields to extract, the shape of the output, the constraints it must satisfy.

## Step 03 — Export

Get the structured output back with a full audit bundle — every field linked to the source span it came from.
