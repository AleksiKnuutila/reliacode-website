---
title: "What does a pilot involve in practice?"
question: "What does a pilot involve in practice?"
sort: 40
---

A pilot typically runs [6–12 weeks] and has three parts: we work with your
team to scope a concrete use case from your real workflow, we configure
and run the system on a representative sample of your documents, and we
evaluate the results together — including where the system performs well,
where it abstains, and where it gets things wrong. You get the outputs,
the evaluation, and direct access to the research team. We get feedback
that shapes the system.
