---
title: "What kinds of organisations are you looking for as pilots?"
question: "What kinds of organisations are you looking for as pilots?"
sort: 30
---

Teams doing document-heavy analytical work where being wrong has real
consequences — underwriting, claims, diligence, compliance, clinical
review, regulatory submissions. The common thread is the work itself:
long, messy, multi-document cases where experts currently spend hours
cross-checking facts and where a confident-but-wrong AI answer is worse
than no answer. We're industry-agnostic, but we're looking for partners
with real volume and a willingness to evaluate the system critically.
